# 🛒 Olist E-commerce Sales & Logistics Analysis
An end-to-end data analytics project investigating 100,000+ orders from 2016-2018 to drive business growth and operational efficiency.


## Business Objectives
As a Product Analyst, I aimed to answer three critical questions:
1. **Revenue Drivers:** Which product categories and regions contribute 80% of the revenue?
2. **Logistics Performance:** What is the gap between estimated vs. actual delivery times across different Brazilian states?
3. **Product Viability:** Identifying categories where freight costs eat into profit margins.

## Tech Stack
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy):** Data cleaning and merging of 8 relational datasets.
- **Power BI:** Interactive data visualization and KPI development.
- **Excel:** Initial data auditing and gap analysis.

## Key Insights & Recommendations
- **The "Black Friday" Effect:** Identified a 300% surge in volume during November 2017. *Recommendation: Scalable logistics planning for Q4 peaks.*
- **Logistics Bottlenecks:** Found that orders in northern states (e.g., Alagoas) take 50% longer than the national average. *Recommendation: Localized micro-fulfillment centers in high-lag regions.*
- **Category Leaders:** Health & Beauty and Bed Bath Table are the top revenue drivers, maintaining consistent month-over-month growth.

##  Project Structure

- `Olist_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`: Full interactive Power BI report.
